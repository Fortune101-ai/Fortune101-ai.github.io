About Me

Junior Web Developer

Hello! I am Fortune Mahloane, a passionate web developer with a unique journey that began at the University of Cape Town, where I studied Mathematics and Computer Science. Although I chose to pursue a different path before completing my degree, my time at university sparked my curiosity for problem-solving and technology. After leaving university, I became a private tutor, teaching Mathematics and Physical Sciences to high school learners. During this time, my interest in web design grew as I envisioned how I could create websites to enhance my tutoring efforts. This inspiration led me to dive into the world of web development, where I found my true passion. I am now proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Bootstrap, Python, and Sass. I love transforming ideas into reality through code and continuously seek to learn new technologies and techniques to enhance my skills. Outside of coding, I enjoy spending time with friends, diving into video games, and following WWE. I believe that balance is key, and these activities inspire my creativity and drive. I am excited to connect with like-minded individuals and contribute to innovative projects that make a positive impact. Let us create something amazing together!
